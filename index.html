— About the project

The pavement doesn't lie.
Every crack
tells a story.

This spot has become home to many like-minded skaters, and over the past year, I've had the opportunity to document how they upcycle old furniture and discarded materials, giving them a second life as skate obstacles. Beyond their creativity, this group is incredibly welcoming, often embracing beginners and fostering a true sense of community here.

A special thanks to @leomontoya for collaborating with me to bring this project the style and excitement it deserves. More to come soon!

— @samhuertas1
